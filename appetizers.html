Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

  • Ingredients: Tomatoes, basil, garlic, olive oil, baguette
  • Steps: Chop tomatoes and basil, mix with garlic and oil, toast baguette slices, top with mixture.

Stuffed Mushrooms

  • Ingredients: Mushrooms, cream cheese, garlic, herbs
  • Steps: Remove stems, mix filling, stuff mushrooms, bake until golden.
