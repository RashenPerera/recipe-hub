Simple Appetizer Recipes

Peanut Butter Banana Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • Optional: honey or chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Slice the banana into small rounds.
  2. Spread peanut butter between two banana slices to make a sandwich.
  3. Top with honey or chocolate chips if desired.

Tomato Cucumber Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Chop the tomato and cucumber into small pieces.
  2. Mix in a bowl with salt, pepper, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
